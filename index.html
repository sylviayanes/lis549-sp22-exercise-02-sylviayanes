What Is It?

Hobbiton is near the city of Matamata, New Zealand, and it is a recreation of a ficitional place in The Lord of the Rings book series by J.R.R Tolkien.

How to Get There?

From the North

  1. Start in Waharoa, New Zealand
  2. Drive south on State Highway 27
  3. Make a lot of right turns

From the South

  1. Start in Putaruru, New Zealand
  2. Drive north on State Highway 1
  3. Make one right turn
  4. Make one left turn
